My Travel Journey

Visiting new countries has immersed me in varied cultures and ways of living. These experiences have been transformative, teaching me invaluable lessons and reshaping my entire outlook on the human experience.

Countries & Places

Saudi Arabia (KSA)

I have spent almost my entire life in Saudi Arabia and continue to call it home. Living here allows me to thrive in a modern environment and interact with diverse communities within this truly unique culture.

Yemen

Spending part of my life in Yemen gave me a firsthand look at its incredible traditions and culture. It is a vital part of my heritage that continues to shape my perspective and sense of self.

Georgia

My visit to Georgia in 2018 allowed me to immerse myself in stunning nature and distinct architectural styles. It was a transformative experience that deepened my interest in global travel and exploration.

UAE (Future Goal)

I aspire to visit the United Arab Emirates, I look forward to discovering its landmarks, immersing myself in its rich heritage, and building unforgettable new memories.

Why Travelling Matters

Traveling helps people understand different cultures, meet new people, and gain valuable life experiences. It encourages growth, independence, and open-minded thinking.
